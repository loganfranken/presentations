Structuring Content

Tip #1

Order Content Meaningfully

Dont

<nav><!-- Main Navigation --></nav>

<aside><!-- Sidebar --></aside>

<h1>Student Resources</h1>

<main><!-- Main Content --></main>

Do

<h1>Student Resources</h1>

<nav><!-- Main Navigation --></nav>

<main><!-- Main Content --></main>

<aside><!-- Sidebar --></aside>

Tip #4

Clear, Descriptive Title

Tip #5

Most Important Part of Title First

Dont

<!-- Which section? -->
<title>Lorem Ipsum Department</title>

<!-- Which website? -->
<title>About</title>

<!-- Important information last -->
<title>Lorem Ispum Department - UCSB - About</title>

Do

<title>
   About - Lorem Ipsum Department
</title>

Headings

Tip #6

Clear and Descriptive

Dont

<h1>This is how to apply</h1>

<h2>First</h2>

<h2>Next</h2>

Do

<h1>How to Apply</h1>

<h2>Before You Apply</h2>

<h2>Step 1: Federal Application</h2>

Tip #7

Only for Actual Headings

Dont

<p>
   Complete the
   <h3>Federal Application</h3>
</p>

Do

<p>
   Complete the
   <strong>Federal Application</strong>
</p>

Tip #8

Nest Headings Properly

Dont

<h1>How to Apply</h1>

<h3>Before You Apply</h3>

<h3>What You Will Need</h3>

Do

<h1>How to Apply</h1>

<h2>Before You Apply</h2>

<h3>What You Will Need</h3>

Tip #9

Only One H1

Dont

<h1>About Us</h1>

<h1>Who Are We?</h1>

<h1>Where Are We Located?</h1>

Do

<h1>About Us</h1>

<h2>Who Are We?</h2>

<h2>Where Are We Located?</h2>

Do (HTML5)

<h1>About Us</h1>

<section>
   <h1>Who Are We?</h1>
<section>

<section>
   <h1>Where Are We Located?</h1>
<section>

Images

Tip #10

Use the "alt" attribute

Do

<img
   src="butterfly.jpg"
   alt="A butterfly sitting on a leaf"
/>

Tip #11

Use the "longdesc" attribute

Do

<img
   src="sales-chart.png"
   alt="A chart of last month's sales"
   longdesc="sales-chart-desc.html"
/>

Do

<img
   src="sales-chart.png"
   alt="A chart of last month's sales"
   longdesc="#sales-chart-desc"
/>

<div id="#sales-chart-desc">
   <!-- Sales Chart Description -->
</div>

Tip #12
